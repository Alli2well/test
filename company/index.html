We Are The Future Of Aviation

Since her founding in 2018 by four amazing Aerospace Engineering Undergrads of the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Wingxtrer stands tall as the most reliable drone logistics company making lifesaving deliveries with the smartest drone fleet.

Wingxtrer's drone technology is designed, built and tested in Kwahu — the most suitable place for drone testing. Extensive flight testing is performed in the mountaineous region and vast plains of Kwahu, enabling us to design the best solution that works in all regions. Wingxtrer operates with local talents in every part of the globe she finds herself.
