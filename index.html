Wingxtrer makes lower skies delivery of critical and lifesaving products precisely where and when they are needed, safely and reliably, every day.

We Are Exceptional

We transform the lower skies into a resource for sustainable mobility & harness this resource to provide instant access to on-demand supplies globally.

Recent advances in logistics — like instant, on-demand delivery — mainly serve those in advanced countries and cities.

Wingxtrer is a transformational change in logistics. We provide equal access to on-demand delivery, globally, and for the deliveries that matter most, even to those in hard-to-reach areas. This is made possible only with our innovative eVTOL drones

Our drone technology is autonomous, fast, reliable and require no takeoff or landing assistance - thus no runway, hand or catapult launch is required. It is designed to takeoff and land even in the smallest space available.
